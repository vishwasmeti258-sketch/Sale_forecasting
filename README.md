# 📈 Sale_forecasting

# 📋 Overview
To make sales forecasting possible Usage of time series model has done and to visualize the predication.
By this Business make decision based on previous trend of sales.

# 📄 Objectives
• To observe relationships between   different metrics.

• To observe Sales on different periods of time and regional wise.

• To forecast upcoming sales using time series

# 🔍 Dataset Description
The dataset contains the following key columns :-


| Region | Country | Order Date | Ship Date | Total Revenue | Total Profit | Unit Price | Sales Channel | Order Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Sub-Saharan Africa | South Africa | 2023-01-15 | 2023-01-20 | $50,000 | $15,000 | $250.00 | Online | High |
| Europe | Germany | 2023-02-10 | 2023-02-12 | $120,000 | $40,000 | $600.00 | Offline | Critical |
| Asia | Vietnam | 2023-03-05 | 2023-03-15 | $30,000 | $8,500 | $120.00 | Online | Medium |
